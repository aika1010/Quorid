DATABASE MANAGEMENT

Keep focus on your business

Many growing companies that implement databases to support advanced software are often unprepared for the complexities of managing an enterprise-class database. The cost of hiring a qualified database administrator can be prohibitive especially for companies with a small technology footprint. Even companies with expertise on staff may have only a single qualified database administrator (DBA), making vacations or illness a very scary time for an organization - and the DBA.

What we offer...

We have Certified Database Administrators that have extensive experience and certifications in Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle, and DB2. All of our services are provided remotely which reduces costs and allows us to have expertise available around the clock. Our DBAs provide custom solutions for:
  1. Staff Augmentation o Vacation Support – If your only DBA is going on vacation, let us mind the store while they’re away
  2. First-tier – Do you want to let your DBA focus on more complex duties? Let us take care of the day-to-day activities
  3. Second tier – If you don’t have a senior DBA on staff, let us take some of the worry away by being available for critical situations
  4. Full database management – You don’t need a DBA at all! We can handle everything for you from end to end
  5. Infrastructure planning – We can work with you to specify and choose hardware appropriate for your organization
  6. Database installations – Creating new databases and instances for your business
  7. Auditing – When you need to keep an eye on security or specific tables
  8. Database upgrades – Is your database out of support? Let us get you on the latest version
  9. Patching – Regular patching is important to stay ahead of bugs and security concerns
  10. Performance Analysis – Keeping the database running smoothly is an ongoing task
  11. Database optimization – Out of the box settings might work at first, but a database needs to get in tune and stay there
  12. Security – Do you know how many users have full access to your database? We can find out and fix it
  13. High Availability – Extended up time requires advanced configurations and expertise
  14. Disaster Recovery – When the tornado hits your data center, how will you get back in business?
  15. Advanced database technologies – Database functionality and performance can be extended with features like pluggable databases, table partitioning, and much more. We can help you understand the benefits and implement a solution
