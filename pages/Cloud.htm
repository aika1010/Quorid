CLOUD AND RECOVERY

Keep focus on your business

It is quite common situation when a server suddenly went down and a large about of important data disappeared. Be sure with QUORID ltd that your valuable data is secure, protected and easily restored whenever you need it.

What we offer...

  1. Cloud-based Disaster Recovery
  2. Recovery for physical and virtual servers as well as Windows and virtual Linux environments
  3. Built-in WAN optimization for data replication
  4. Sub-five minute failover to recovered systems and applications
  5. Secure VPN connectivity to your servers in a private cloud
  6. SSL encryption in flight, AES encryption at rest
  7. Failback to on-premise environments from the cloud
    8. 24x7x365 US-based engineering level support to assist with onboarding, failover, and failback

How we work...

We will replicate your network in the cloud and in case of emergency you will have VPN access to your recovered environment. Before setting up a business relationship, we will understand your IT needs, capabilities, limitations and budget.
