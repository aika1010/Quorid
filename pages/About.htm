About The QUORID

worldwide complete IT solution for your business

QUORID has been established in 2013 with a group of high level professionals to deliver a broad range of analytical services and web-based products, designed to meet the needs of the front and back offices at some of the most well-known companies across the globe.

We spend our working days providing the highest possible quality services and reliable delivery. This means supporting our clients to build efficient and functional businesses and operational workflow through a comprehensive set of services, such as evaluation the emerging markets fixed-income securities, maintaining data analysis and developing analytical models to facilitate investment in emerging markets, statistical analysis for market research and graphical presentations, translating complex data into reports and flexible web-based visualization, processing data and efficient database design.
